Welcome to Probe Right Inspection

Welcome to our Services page! At ProbeRight Home Inspections, we offer a wide range of inspection services to ensure your home is safe, sound, and in excellent condition. Explore our comprehensive offerings below to see how we can assist you with all your inspection needs.

Essential Services

Pre-Purchase Home Inspections: Before you buy, ensure your dream home is in top condition. Our thorough inspections identify any existing or potential issues, providing you with peace of mind.

Pre-Listing Home Inspections: Get ahead of the game by identifying and addressing problems before listing your property. Our pre-listing inspections help you sell with confidence.

New Construction Inspections: Ensure your newly built home meets all required standards and codes. Our inspections verify that everything is up to par, giving you a worry-free move-in experience.

Warranty Inspections: Before your home's warranty expires, our inspections identify any issues that the builder should address, protecting your investment.

Value-Added Services

Thermal Imaging: Use infrared cameras to detect hidden issues such as leaks, insulation problems, and electrical hotspots.

Infrared Inspections: Uncover hidden problems in your home using advanced infrared technology.

Maintenance Inspections: Schedule regular check-ups to keep your home in good condition and catch any issues early.

Detailed Inspection Reports: Receive comprehensive, easy-to-understand reports with our findings and recommendations.
